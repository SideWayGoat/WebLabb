This Is Me

Born in the summer of 1995 i've lived all of my life in or in the area around Varberg. My earliest memory is of me bonking my head on the table to make my little sister laugh.
My love of computer came one time when visiting my uncle and saw him playing WoW: Classic just after release, before that I don't think I had a love for it only a tiny interest. I was however never allowed to play WoW said my mother so I spent my time in other games like CS 1.6, Battlefield, WC3 among others. I've spent my whole life infront of the computer and on occation wondered how it works so this is a logical and fun step to finally take, learning to write code is frustrating at times but really fun.
